For my thesis, I performed sentiment analysis on earnings calls for 30 companies in the Dow Jones over a period of 10 years. I then built some visual tools allowing users to explore the findings.

The analysis was done in R and the visualization was built in D3.js. It can be viewed on a subsection of this site .
