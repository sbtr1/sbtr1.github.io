Here are some quotes I think about from time to time that help me navigate the market. They're applicable for a large range of situations and we should do well to apply them to other aspects of our lives:

  • "When nothing seems to help, I go and look at a stonecutter hammering away at his rock perhaps a hundred times without as much as a crack showing in it. Yet at the hundred and first blow it will split in two, and I know it was not that blow that did it, but all that had gone before." -- Jacob Riis
  • "The first rule is to keep an untroubled spirit. The second is to look things in the face and know them for what they are." -- Marcus Aurelius, Meditations
  • "We talk a lot about process — not outcome — and trying to consistently take all the best information you can and consistently make good decisions. Sometimes they work and sometimes they don't, but you reevaluate them all." -- Sam Hinkie
  • "There's levels to it, you and I know... be humble." -- Kendrick Lamar
